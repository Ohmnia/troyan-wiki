# AYE Faction

Top faction aesthetic:

```txt
Clean
Advanced
Post-material
Elegant
Synthetic
Holographic
Precision-engineered
Near-utopian
```

Visual themes:

* white ceramic armor
* cyan energy
* pale metallics
* translucent holographics
* sleek geometry
* anti-gravity feel
* clean vertical architecture
* intelligent systems
* high-tech but calm

This faction should feel:

```txt
Divine Technology
+
Clinical Intelligence
+
Controlled Power
```

Industrial

* corroded metals
* sickly organic growths

# IMPORTANT DESIGN RECOMMENDATION

Do NOT make the themes:

```txt
pure white
or
pure black
```

Use:

```txt
soft atmospheric tones
```

This keeps the site readable during long sessions.
